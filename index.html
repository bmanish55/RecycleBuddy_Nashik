Bin Classification

As per Nashik Municipal Corporation guidelines, please use the following bins for waste disposal. This ensures proper sorting and enhances recycling efficiency.

Blue Bin

For **Paper & Card**.

Yellow Bin

For **Plastics & Packaging**.

Brown Bin

For **Bio/Organic** waste.

Green Igloos

For **Glass** (by color).

Gray Bin

For **Residual** waste.
